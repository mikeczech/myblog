Recently, I’ve been thinking about how AI coding agents enable us to do things we couldn’t realistically do before. We spend a lot of time talking about replacing software engineers or doing the same things faster, but I find the new things AI makes possible much more interesting.

I’m a big fan of a [show and tell culture](https://tulv.io/stories/why-we-show-and-tell) instead of endless discussions about abstract ideas. I’d like to build a safe environment where people feel comfortable showing incomplete work, know it’s okay not to be great at presenting, and are proud of small achievements.

I’ve noticed that AI makes it much easier to show ongoing work that would have stayed hidden before. Quick visualizations, throwaway tools, and small interactive demos are suddenly worth building because they only take minutes. As a result, presentations have become much more lively. We end up discussing something tangible instead of imagining it.
