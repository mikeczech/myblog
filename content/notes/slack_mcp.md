One of my favourite new tricks is to be a little more verbose in Slack and then use Claude and [Slack MCP](https://slack.com/intl/en-gb/help/articles/48855576908307-Guide-to-the-Slack-MCP-server) to generate a pull request from the discussion. That way, ideas from our Slack discussions make their way into the product almost immediately!

It’s a small example of how software engineering is becoming less about producing code and more about collaboratively shaping the product.
